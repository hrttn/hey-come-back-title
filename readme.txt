== Description ==
 
# Bring your users back to your website when they switch tab!

**Hey Come Back! Title** allows you bring your users back to your website when they switch the tab.
You will be able to gain their focus back and finish reading your article or complete their purchase.
 
== Installation ==
 
## From within your WordPress website
1. Visit *Plugins > Add New*
1. Search for "Hey Come Back! Title"
1. Install the plugin and activate it.
1. That's it, whenever you or your users leave one page on your website, the title will be flashing "Hey Come Back".

## Manually through FTP
1. Download the plugin and the folder hey-come-back-title
1. Upload the hey-come-back-title folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory through FTP
1. Activate the plugin in the *Plugins* menu in your WordPress website.

## Manually
1. Download the plugin and the zipped file hey-come-back-title$
1. Visit *Plugins > Add New*
1. Upload the hey-come-back-title zip file
1. Activate the plugin in the *Plugins* menu in your WordPress website.
